<p>Hermetism from Late Antiquity to Humanism (Instrumenta Patristica et Mediaevalia 40) is a scholarly volume of conference proceedings published by Brepols, edited by Paolo Lucentini, Ilaria Parri, and Vittoria Perrone Compagni. It collects the papers delivered at the international conference organized by the Hermes Latinus research group in Naples from November 20 to 24, 2001.</p><p>The volume is organized into four thematic sections: Philosophical Hermetism, Medieval and Renaissance Hermetism, Arabic and Hebrew Hermetism, and Operative Hermetism (covering alchemy, astrology, and magic).
