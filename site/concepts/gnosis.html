Gnosis (Saving Knowledge) THEOLOGICAL

γνῶσις, salvific knowledge

Experiential knowledge of divine nature and cosmic structure; transformative recognition of self as divine.

Hermetic gnosis is not mere intellectual knowing but transformative recognition. It is 'knowledge of self' — self-realization of divine nature achieved through interior illumination and initiatory practice. Unlike Gnostic gnosis which rejects the material world, Hermetic gnosis affirms the cosmos as good and knowable.

Significance

Central salvific mechanism distinguishing Hermeticism from both Gnosticism (different attitude to cosmos) and Christianity (different salvific mechanism).
