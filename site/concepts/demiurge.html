Demiurge (Craftsman God) COSMOLOGICAL

δημιουργός, Creator

Second-order creative deity responsible for shaping the material world.

In Hermetic cosmology, the Demiurge is generally a benevolent craftsman who shapes matter according to divine patterns. This contrasts sharply with Gnostic systems where the demiurge (Yaldabaoth) is ignorant or malevolent. The Hermetic Demiurge creates a beautiful, ordered cosmos worthy of contemplation.

Significance

Key distinction between Hermeticism and Gnosticism. The positive valuation of the Demiurge entails a positive valuation of the material world, enabling rather than prohibiting engagement with nature (alchemy, science).
