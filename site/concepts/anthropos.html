Anthropos (Primal Human) PHILOSOPHICAL

Divine Humanity, Ἄνθρωπος

Original archetypal human containing the microcosm of all creation; human nature as essentially divine.

In Poimandres (CH I), the Anthropos is a divine being who descends into matter through desire for creation. Humans retain this divine nature and can recover it through gnosis. This anthropology grounds the Hermetic claim that humans are capable of deification (theopoiesis).

Significance

Basis for human capacity for salvation and transformation. Foundation of Hermetic magical anthropocentrism — the magus can affect the cosmos because the human contains the cosmos.

Appears In

Poimandres (Corpus Hermeticum I)
