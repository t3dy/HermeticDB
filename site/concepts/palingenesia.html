Palingenesia (Regeneration) THEOLOGICAL

spiritual rebirth, new birth, παλιγγενεσία

Spiritual rebirth through shedding material identification and assuming divine consciousness.

Central to CH XIII, where Hermes instructs Tat on the initiatory process. The practitioner sheds the twelve torments of materiality and receives the ten divine powers. Jean-Pierre Mahé identifies this as following van Gennep's initiatory structure: separation, liminality, incorporation.

Significance

The transformative endpoint of Hermetic practice. Connects to alchemical symbolism of death and rebirth (nigredo → rubedo).
