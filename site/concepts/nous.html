Nous (Divine Mind) PHILOSOPHICAL

Divine Intelligence, νοῦς

The supreme intellect; first principle of being below the One; consciousness as cosmic principle.

In Hermetic metaphysics, Nous is the creator of the cosmos through thought and the vehicle of divine knowledge transmitted to humanity. In Poimandres (CH I), Nous reveals itself as the source of all creation. The goal of Hermetic gnosis is recognition of one's essential identity with Nous.

Significance

Core Hermetic concept appearing in virtually every treatise. The term's frequency and semantic range are key markers for computational analysis of Hermetic texts.
