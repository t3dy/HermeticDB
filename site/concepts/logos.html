Logos (Divine Word) PHILOSOPHICAL

Divine Reason, Creative Speech, λόγος

Rational principle of creation; divine utterance that structures reality; intermediary between transcendent One and material cosmos.

In Hermetic cosmology, Logos emanates from Nous and structures material creation. It is both the rational order of the cosmos and the creative speech by which that order is established. Can be perceived and known by initiates who achieve gnosis.

Significance

Mediating principle between the transcendent and material in Hermetic philosophy. Connects Hermetic thought to Stoic, Platonic, and Christian Logos traditions.

Appears In

Corpus Hermeticum Poimandres (Corpus Hermeticum I)
