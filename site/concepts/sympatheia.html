Sympatheia (Universal Correspondence) COSMOLOGICAL

cosmic sympathy, συμπάθεια

Universal resonance and correspondence between all levels of reality; the ability of lower to affect upper through right knowledge.

The Stoic-Hermetic principle that all parts of the cosmos are interconnected through sympathetic relationships. This interconnection allows for the efficacy of astrology (celestial influence on terrestrial events), talismanic magic (material objects channeling celestial forces), and theurgy (human ritual affecting divine presence).

Significance

Basis of all operative Hermeticism. Without sympatheia, alchemy, astrology, and magic would have no theoretical justification.
