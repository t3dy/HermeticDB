Amphitheatrum Sapientiae Aeternae TREATISE

LATIN · TREATISE · 1595–1609 CE

Illustrated alchemical and theosophical work by Heinrich Khunrath. Features elaborate engravings linking the Emerald Tablet to VITRIOL acrostic and deep spiritual introspection.

Transmission

First published 1595, expanded edition 1609. Cemented the Tablet's status as the ultimate mystical and chemical icon.
