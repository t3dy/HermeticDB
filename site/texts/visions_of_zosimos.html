GREEK · PRIMARY_SOURCE · 250–310 CE

Allegorical dream-visions by Zosimos of Panopolis encoding alchemical operations within cosmological and mystical narrative. Features the sacrificing priest Ion, the 15-step altar, and seven punishments. Most elaborate visionary alchemy in the late antique corpus.
