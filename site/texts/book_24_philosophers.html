Book of the 24 Philosophers (Liber XXIV Philosophorum) TREATISE

LATIN · TREATISE · 300–1200 CE

Liber XXIV Philosophorum

Collection of 24 definitions of God in aphoristic form. Possibly late antique origin, earliest manuscripts 12th century. Contains the famous definition 'God is an infinite sphere whose center is everywhere and circumference is nowhere.' Influential in medieval philosophy.
