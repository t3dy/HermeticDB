Three Books of Occult Philosophy (De occulta philosophia) TREATISE

LATIN · TREATISE · 1510–1533 CE

De occulta philosophia libri tres

Cornelius Agrippa's systematic synthesis of Hermetic, Neoplatonic, Kabbalistic, and astrological magic. The most influential early modern magical treatise, providing the theoretical framework for all subsequent Western ceremonial magic.
