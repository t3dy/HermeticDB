Second Book of the Elements of Foundation TREATISE

ARABIC · TREATISE · 850–950 CE

كتاب أسطقس الأس الثاني

Alchemical treatise attributed to Jabir ibn Hayyan containing an early Arabic version of the Emerald Tablet. Found by Eric Holmyard. Part of the massive Jabirian corpus, likely a collective pseudepigraphical effort by scholars of possible Isma'ili persuasion.

Transmission

Part of the Jabirian corpus. Holmyard was the first to find this version of the Emerald Tablet.
