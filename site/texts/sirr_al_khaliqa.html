Book of the Secret of Creation (Kitab Sirr al-Khaliqa) ENCYCLOPEDIA

ARABIC · ENCYCLOPEDIA · 650–833 CE

كتاب سر الخليقة وصنعة الطبيعة

Expansive cosmological encyclopedia attributed to Balinas (Apollonius of Tyana). Contains the earliest known contextual embedding of the Emerald Tablet. Structured into six books following creation sequence: God, celestial spheres, minerals, plants, animals, humans. Two recensions: A (shorter, closer to Greek original) and B (expanded).

Transmission

Also known as Kitab Balaniyus al-Hakim fi'l-Ilal (Book of Wise Balinas on the Causes). Over 30 manuscripts across major libraries. Weisser (1979) critical edition based on 17 manuscripts.
