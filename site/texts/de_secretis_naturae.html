On the Secrets of Nature (De secretis naturae) TRANSLATION

LATIN · TRANSLATION · 1145–1151 CE

De secretis naturae

First known Latin translation of the complete Kitab Sirr al-Khaliqa by Hugo of Santalla. Preserves the cosmological context and frame narrative of the Arabic original. Did not achieve widespread circulation compared to later recensions.

Transmission

Hugo's version uses 'Superiora de inferioribus, inferiora de superioribus' rather than the vulgate's 'Quod est inferius est sicut quod est superius'.
