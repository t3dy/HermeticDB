Corpus Hermeticum PRIMARY_SOURCE

GREEK · PRIMARY_SOURCE · 100–300 CE

Collection of 18 Greek philosophical-theological treatises attributed to Hermes Trismegistus. Core texts of the philosophical Hermetic tradition covering cosmogony, theology, soteriology, and the nature of the divine mind (Nous). Compiled ca. 4th century CE from earlier individual treatises.
