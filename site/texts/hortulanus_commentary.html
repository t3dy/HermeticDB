Hortulanus Commentary (Liber super textum Hermetis) COMMENTARY

LATIN · COMMENTARY · 1300–1350 CE

Liber super textum Hermetis

The most authoritative medieval exegesis of the Emerald Tablet. Hortulanus interprets the verses as a sequential laboratory manual for creating the Philosopher's Stone, decoding sun/moon imagery through sulfur-mercury theory, and reading the 'separation' as mundification via gentle heat.

Transmission

Most widely disseminated commentary. Anchored the Tablet within practical laboratory chemistry.
