Book of the Silvery Water and the Starry Earth TREATISE

ARABIC · TREATISE · 900–960 CE

Alchemical work by Ibn Umayl containing a distinct version of the Emerald Tablet discovery narrative. In this version, an alchemical stone table rests across the knees of Hermes's corpse inside an Egyptian pyramid, inscribed with hieroglyphic writing described as birbawi.

Transmission

Provides a unique variant of the discovery narrative linking the Tablet to Egyptian pyramid traditions.
