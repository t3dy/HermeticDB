LATIN · PSEUDO_EPIGRAPHA · 1100–1200 CE

De sex rerum principiis

Medieval treatise attributed to Hermes Trismegistus on six fundamental principles. Adapted Neoplatonic cosmology for medieval Latin readership. Edited by Paolo Lucentini and Mark Damien Delp.
