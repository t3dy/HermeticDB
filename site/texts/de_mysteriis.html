De mysteriis (On the Mysteries) TREATISE

GREEK · TREATISE · 300–330 CE

Περὶ τῶν μυστηρίων

Iamblichus's defense of theurgy against Porphyry's rationalist critique. Treats Hermetic texts as translations of Egyptian Books of Thoth. Argues that ritual language carries inherent divine energy that fades through translation (exitalos). Key text linking Hermetic and Neoplatonic traditions.
