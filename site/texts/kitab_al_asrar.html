Book of Secrets (Kitab al-Asrar) TREATISE

ARABIC · TREATISE · 850–925 CE

كتاب الأسرار

Alchemical treatise attributed to Al-Razi (Rhazes) describing chemical operations and transmutation. Major work in the Arabic alchemical tradition synthesizing Greek chemical philosophy with Arabic experimentation.
