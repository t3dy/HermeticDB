Picatrix (Ghayat al-Hakim) COMPILATION

ARABIC · COMPILATION · 950–1000 CE

غاية الحكيم

Comprehensive Arabic treatise on talismanic magic, celestial invocation, and Hermetic-Neoplatonic cosmology. Major conduit for transmission of Hermetic magical practice into medieval Europe via Latin translation.
