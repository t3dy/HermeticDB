Book of Treasures COMPILATION

SYRIAC · COMPILATION · 800–900 CE

Syriac compilation by Job of Odessa (Ayyub al-Ruhawi) showing conceptual similarities with the Arabic Sirr al-Khaliqa. Part of the evidence for Syriac intermediary traditions in the Emerald Tablet's transmission.

Transmission

Suggests a possible Syriac source for the Sirr al-Khaliqa, though none of the Syriac writings contain the Tablet itself.
