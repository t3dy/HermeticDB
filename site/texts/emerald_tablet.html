Emerald Tablet (Tabula Smaragdina) PRIMARY_SOURCE

ARABIC · PRIMARY_SOURCE · 600–750 CE

اللوح الزمردي / Tabula Smaragdina

The seminal theoretical document of Western and Islamicate alchemical traditions. A compact, cryptic text attributed to Hermes Trismegistus, encoding a synthesis of Aristotelian natural physics and Neoplatonic emanationist cosmology. Earliest known context is within the Arabic Kitab Sirr al-Khaliqa.

Transmission

Entered Latin Europe through Arabic science via multiple 12th-century translation vectors. Circulated both embedded in larger works and as a standalone text.
