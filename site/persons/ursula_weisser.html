Ursula Weisser SCHOLAR

20th c. · SCHOLAR

Produced the critical edition of the Sirr al-Khaliqa (1979, Aleppo), analyzing over 20 manuscripts and establishing two recensions (A and B). Originating as Frankfurt dissertation (1974) under Fuat Sezgin.
