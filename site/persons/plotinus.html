Plotinus PHILOSOPHER

3rd c. CE · 204–270 · PHILOSOPHER

Founder of Neoplatonism. Developed the emanationist metaphysics (the One, Nous, Soul, Matter) that became the philosophical backdrop for Hermetic cosmology. Teacher of Porphyry. His mystical ascent theory parallels Hermetic gnosis.
