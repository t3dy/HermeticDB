Lodovico Lazzarelli AUTHOR

late 15th c. CE · 1447–1500 · AUTHOR

Also known as: Lazarelli

Italian Hermetic philosopher who represents the bridge between medieval and Renaissance Hermeticism. Author of Hermetic writings studied in the ACMRS edition by Hanegraaff and Bouthoorn. Active renovator of the Hermetic tradition.
