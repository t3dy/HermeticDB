Philip of Tripoli TRANSLATOR

13th c. CE · TRANSLATOR

Also known as: Philippus Tripolitanus

Completed a full Latin translation of the Secretum Secretorum c. 1243. Because of Aristotle's unassailable authority, this became one of the most widely read books of the High Middle Ages.

Key Events
