mythical · MYTHICAL_FIGURE

Also known as: Hermes Thrice-Great, Thoth, هرمس المثلث بالحكمة

Syncretic Hellenistic figure combining Greek Hermes and Egyptian Thoth. Traditional author of the Emerald Tablet. In the Sirr al-Khaliqa frame narrative, his tomb contains the tablet inscribed with primordial secrets of nature.
