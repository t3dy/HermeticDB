Al-Razi (Rhazes) AUTHOR

9th-10th c. CE · 854–925 · AUTHOR

Also known as: Abu Bakr Muhammad ibn Zakariyya al-Razi, Rhazes

Major Islamic alchemist and physician. Author of the Book of Secrets (Kitab al-Asrar). Synthesized Greek chemical philosophy with Arabic experimentation. One of the most important figures in the Arabic alchemical tradition.
