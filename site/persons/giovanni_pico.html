Giovanni Pico della Mirandola PHILOSOPHER

late 15th c. CE · 1463–1494 · PHILOSOPHER

Also known as: Pico

Florentine humanist who synthesized Hermetic, Kabbalistic, Neoplatonic, and Christian doctrines in his 900 Theses (1486). Radicalized Ficino's synthesis by adding Kabbalah. His Oration on the Dignity of Man draws on Hermetic anthropology.
