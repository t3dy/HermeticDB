Thomas Aquinas PHILOSOPHER

13th c. CE · 1225–1274 · PHILOSOPHER

Scholastic theologian whose Aristotelian framework filtered and controlled the reception of Hermetic ideas in medieval universities. Acknowledged Hermetic authority selectively while restricting magical applications.
