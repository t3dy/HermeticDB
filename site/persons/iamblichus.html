Iamblichus PHILOSOPHER

late 3rd-early 4th c. CE · 245–325 · PHILOSOPHER

Also known as: Iamblichus of Chalcis, Abamon

Syrian Neoplatonic philosopher and theurgist. Author of De mysteriis, defending ritual practice against Porphyry's rationalism. Adopted the pseudonym Abamon (Egyptian prophet persona) to present himself as custodian of Hermetic-Egyptian tradition. Treated Hermetic texts as translations of Egyptian Books of Thoth.
