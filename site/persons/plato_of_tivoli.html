Plato of Tivoli TRANSLATOR

12th c. CE · TRANSLATOR

Also known as: Plato Tiburtinus

Italian mathematician and astronomer who likely produced the Vulgate Latin Emerald Tablet (c. 1134-1145) in Barcelona. Attribution conjectural but widely accepted.

Key Events

1140 — Vulgate Latin Emerald Tablet produced
Plato of Tivoli (probably) produces the standalone Latin Emerald Tablet in Barcelona. This becomes the dominant European version.
