Jabir ibn Hayyan AUTHOR

8th-9th c. CE · 721–815 · AUTHOR

Also known as: Geber, جابر بن حيان

Foundational figure of Islamic alchemy. The massive Jabirian corpus (hundreds of treatises) was likely a collective pseudepigraphical effort, possibly of Isma'ili persuasion (Kraus). The Emerald Tablet appears in the Kitab Ustuqus al-Uss attributed to him.
