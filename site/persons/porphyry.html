Porphyry PHILOSOPHER

late 3rd c. CE · 234–305 · PHILOSOPHER

Also known as: Porphyry of Tyre

Plotinian Neoplatonist and critic of ritual/theurgy. His Letter to Anebo questioned the efficacy of traditional rites, prompting Iamblichus's De mysteriis response. Represents the rationalist opposition to Hermetic-theurgic traditions.
