Isaac Casaubon SCHOLAR

late 16th-early 17th c. CE · 1559–1614 · SCHOLAR

Classical scholar who in 1614 demonstrated that the Corpus Hermeticum was not ancient Egyptian but post-Christian in date, based on linguistic and stylistic analysis. His dating destroyed the Renaissance belief in Hermetic prisca theologia but did not end Hermetic influence.
