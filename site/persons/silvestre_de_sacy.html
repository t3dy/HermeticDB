Silvestre de Sacy SCHOLAR

late 18th-early 19th c. · 1758–1838 · SCHOLAR

Also known as: Antoine-Isaac Silvestre de Sacy

French orientalist who in 1799 demonstrated the connection between the Sirr al-Khaliqa and Apollonius of Tyana, correcting the earlier misidentification as an Arabic extract of Pliny.
