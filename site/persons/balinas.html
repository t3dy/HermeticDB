Balinas (Pseudo-Apollonius of Tyana) MYTHICAL_FIGURE

1st c. CE (historical) / 8th-9th c. (textual persona) · MYTHICAL_FIGURE

Also known as: Apollonius of Tyana, بلينس, Apollonius Tyanaeus

Arabic rendering of the neo-Pythagorean philosopher Apollonius of Tyana. In Arabic sources, revered as 'Lord of the Talismans' (sahib al-tilasmat). Attributed discoverer/transmitter of the Emerald Tablet in the Sirr al-Khaliqa frame narrative.

Associated Texts

Key Events

650 — Sirr al-Khaliqa composed/compiled
The cosmological encyclopedia attributed to Balinas is written, containing the earliest known contextual embedding of the Emerald Tablet.
