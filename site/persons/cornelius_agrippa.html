Cornelius Agrippa AUTHOR

early 16th c. CE · 1486–1535 · AUTHOR

Also known as: Heinrich Cornelius Agrippa von Nettesheim

German magus and systematic thinker who produced the most influential early modern magical synthesis in Three Books of Occult Philosophy (1533). Student of Trithemius. Integrated Hermetic, Neoplatonic, Kabbalistic, and astrological systems into a coherent magical worldview.
