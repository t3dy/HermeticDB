Augustine of Hippo PHILOSOPHER

late 4th-early 5th c. CE · 354–430 · PHILOSOPHER

Also known as: Saint Augustine, Augustinus

Church father who critically engaged with Hermetic texts. In City of God, both praised Hermes as a pagan who recognized the true God and condemned the Asclepius for its account of statue animation as demonic idolatry. Key figure in the Christian reception and suppression of Hermeticism.
