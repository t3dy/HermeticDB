Giordano Bruno PHILOSOPHER

late 16th c. CE · 1548–1600 · PHILOSOPHER

Also known as: Bruno

Italian philosopher who radicalized Hermetic naturalism toward pantheism and infinite cosmology. Author of De magia and Heroic Frenzies. Burned at the stake in 1600. Represents the most extreme development of Renaissance Hermeticism.
