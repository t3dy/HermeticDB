Marsilio Ficino TRANSLATOR

15th c. CE · 1433–1499 · TRANSLATOR

Also known as: Ficino

Florentine Neoplatonist who translated the Corpus Hermeticum into Latin (1463) before completing his translation of Plato, reflecting the priority given to Hermetic wisdom. Founder of the Platonic Academy. His Platonic Theology integrated Hermeticism with Christianity. The central figure in the Renaissance revival of Hermeticism.
