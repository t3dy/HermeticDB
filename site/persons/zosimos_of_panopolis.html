Zosimos of Panopolis AUTHOR

ca. 300 CE · AUTHOR

Also known as: Zosimos

Greco-Egyptian alchemist and compiler of the Cheirometica (28-book chemical encyclopedia). Author of the allegorical Visions encoding alchemical operations within mystical narrative. Bridge between Hermetic philosophy and practical alchemy. Cited Demokritos and Maria as authorities.
