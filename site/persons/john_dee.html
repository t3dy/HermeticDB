John Dee PHILOSOPHER

16th c. CE · 1527–1608 · PHILOSOPHER

English mathematician, astrologer, and Hermetic magus. Court philosopher to Elizabeth I. His Monas Hieroglyphica attempted to create a universal symbolic system drawing on Hermetic, Kabbalistic, and mathematical principles.
