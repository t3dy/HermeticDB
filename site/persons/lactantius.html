Lactantius AUTHOR

late 3rd-early 4th c. CE · 250–325 · AUTHOR

Also known as: Lucius Caecilius Firmianus Lactantius

Early Christian apologist who cited Hermes Trismegistus approvingly as a pagan prophet who foresaw Christian truths. His use of Hermetic texts in Divinae Institutiones established the model for Christian appropriation of Hermetic authority.
