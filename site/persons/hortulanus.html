Hortulanus COMMENTATOR

early 14th c. CE · COMMENTATOR

Also known as: Ortolanus

Obscure but highly influential alchemist who wrote the most authoritative medieval commentary on the Emerald Tablet (Liber super textum Hermetis), interpreting it as a sequential laboratory manual for creating the Philosopher's Stone.
