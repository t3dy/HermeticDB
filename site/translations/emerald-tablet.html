V. Arabic (Sirr al-Khaliqa)
ARABIC		 Latin Vulgate
LATIN		 Newton's English Translation
ENGLISH
0
1 حقًّا يقينًا لا شك فيه Verum, sine mendacio, certum et verissimum: Tis true without lying, certain & most true.
2 إنّ الأعلى من الأسفل، والأسفل من الأعلى Quod est inferius est sicut quod est superius, et quod est superius est sicut quod est inferius, ad perpetranda miracula rei unius. That wch is below is like that wch is above & that wch is above is like yt wch is below to do ye miracles of one only thing.
3 يعمل عجائب شيء واحد وكما كانت الأشياء كلها من واحد بتدبير واحد فكذلك ولدت من هذا الشيء الواحد بتدبير Et sicut omnes res fuerunt ab uno, meditatione unius, sic omnes res natae fuerunt ab hac una re, adaptatione. And as all things have been & arose from one by ye mediation of one: so all things have their birth from this one thing by adaptation.
4 أبوه الشمس، وأمه القمر Pater eius est Sol, mater eius Luna; The Sun is its father, the moon its mother,
5 حملته الريح في بطنها، أرضته الأرض portavit illud ventus in ventre suo; nutrix eius terra est. the wind hath carried it in its belly, the earth its nourse.
6 هو أبو كل طلسم في العالم كله Pater omnis telesmi totius mundi est hic. The father of all perfection in ye whole world is here.
6a قوته تامّة إذا تحولت إلى الأرض Vis eius integra est, si versa fuerit in terram.
7 تفصل الأرض من النار، اللطيف من الكثيف برفق وحكمة Separabis terram ab igne, subtile a spisso, suaviter cum magno ingenio. Its force or power is entire if it be converted into earth.
7a Seperate thou ye earth from ye fire, ye subtile from the gross sweetly wth great indoustry.
8 يصعد من الأرض إلى السماء، ثم ينزل إلى الأرض فيأخذ قوة العلويات والسفليات Ascendit a terra in caelum, iterumque descendit in terram, et recipit vim superiorum et inferiorum. It ascends from ye earth to ye heaven & again it desends to ye earth and receives ye force of things superior & inferior.
9 فبهذا يكون لك مجد العالم كله لذلك يهرب منك كل ظلمة Sic habebis gloriam totius mundi; ideo fugiet a te omnis obscuritas. By this means you shall have ye glory of ye whole world & thereby all obscurity shall fly from you.
10 هذه قوة القوى كلها، لأنها تغلب كل شيء لطيف وتدخل في كل شيء كثيف Haec est totius fortitudinis fortitudo fortis, quia vincet omnem rem subtilem, omnemque solidam penetrabit. Its force is above all force. ffor it vanquishes every subtile thing & penetrates every solid thing.
11 هكذا خلق العالم Sic mundus creatus est.
11a So was ye world created.
12 من هذا تكون العجائب، ووسائله هذه Hinc erunt adaptationes mirabiles, quarum modus est hic. From this are & do come admirable adaptaions whereof ye means (Or process) is here in this.
13 ولذلك سميت هرمس مثلث بالحكمة Itaque vocatus sum Hermes Trismegistus, habens tres partes philosophiae totius mundi. Hence I am called Hermes Trismegist, having the three parts of ye philosophy of ye whole world.
14 Completum est quod dixi de operatione Solis. That wch I have said of ye operation of ye Sun is accomplished & ended.
