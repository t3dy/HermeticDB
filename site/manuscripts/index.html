Manuscripts

Arundel MS 164
British Library, London
early 15th c. · LATIN
Alchemical manuscript collection compiled by Augustinian friars in Nuremberg containing the Vulgate Latin Emerald Tablet.

Preserves the Vulgate Latin variant used by Albertus Magnus and Roger Bacon.

Hermetis Trismegisti Phoenicum Aegyptiorum
Sächsische Landesbibliothek - Staats- und Universitätsbibliothek Dresden, Dresden
1610 · LATIN
Early modern Latin printed edition of Hermetic texts associated with Hermes Trismegistus.

Represents the early modern print culture engagement with Hermetic philosophy.

Keynes MS 28
King's College Library, Cambridge
c. 1680 · ENGLISH
Isaac Newton's English translation and detailed commentary on the Emerald Tablet.

Vollers 832
Leipzig University Library, Leipzig
Major manuscript of the Sirr al-Khaliqa containing the Emerald Tablet in its original cosmological context.

One of the key Arabic manuscript witnesses used by Weisser in her critical edition.
